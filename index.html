Eco Way to a Waste-Free World Wide Web

♻️ How to Dispose

Locate certified e-waste centers or drop-off points. Never throw electronics in the trash!

You can find nearby centers on your city's waste management portal.

🎨 Upcycling Idea

Turn old phones into security cams or music players!

Apps like AlfredCamera let you turn old phones into home security cams easily.

☠️ Toxic Alert

Electronics contain mercury, lead, and cadmium — harmful to soil and water.

These can cause serious health and environmental hazards if not disposed properly.

⭐ Eco Score

Saving 1 phone = Preventing 55kg of CO₂ emissions!

Reducing e-waste also reduces the need for mining raw materials like lithium and cobalt.
