🍌 Organic Waste (e.g., Banana Peel)

♻️ How to Dispose:

Compost it at home or use a local composting facility.

🎨 Upcycling Idea:

Use peels for natural fertilizer or shine leaves!

☠️ Toxic Alert:

Can attract pests if not disposed properly.

⭐ Eco Score:

Composting reduces methane emissions and enriches soil!
